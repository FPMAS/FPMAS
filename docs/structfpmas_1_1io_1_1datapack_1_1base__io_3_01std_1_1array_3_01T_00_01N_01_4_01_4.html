inlinestatic

Writes array to the data_pack buffer at the given offset. pack_size(array) bytes are written, and offset is incremented accordingly.

pair.first and pair.second are written contiguously using the datapack::write() specializations for T1 and T2.

Items are written contiguously using the datapack::write() specialization for T.
